 <h2>About Me</h2>
 <img src="WIN_20250911_14_43_23_Pro.jpg" alt="Portrait of Diego Grageda" width="400">
 <p>
Hello! My name is Diego Grageda, and I’m a student at Montgomery College pursuing a blend of computer science and game design. 
I’m passionate about immersive storytelling, historical aesthetics, and using technology to make a difference. 
Japan’s culture and landscapes have deeply inspired my creative work—from Edo-period art to modern cityscapes.
 </p>
 <h2>Why I Love Japan</h2>
 <p>
Japan is a country of breathtaking contrasts—where ancient temples sit beside futuristic skyscrapers. 
I’m drawn to its rich history, seasonal beauty, and the harmony between nature and design. 
Whether it’s walking through snowy streets in Hokkaido, admiring cherry blossoms in Tokyo, or exploring quiet shrines in Nara, every 
corner offers something unique. 
The culture is deeply respectful and artistic, which resonates with my own creative values. 
Japan isn’t just a place to visit—it’s a place that fuels imagination and reflection.
 </p>
